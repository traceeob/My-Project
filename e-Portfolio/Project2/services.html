Sports Massage Developed to help with muscle systems used for a particular sport, and uses a variety of approaches to help athletes in training. Useful for promoting flexibilty and helping to prevent injuries, or even help muscle strains, aiding healing after a sports injury. $35 for .5 hours

Shiatsu Massage Meaning "finger pressure" in Japanese, Shiatsu uses varied, rhythmic pressure on certain precise points of the body. Called acupressure points and believed to be important for the flow of the body's vital energy (chi), shiatsu can help relieve blockages at these acupressure points. $50 for 1 hour
$80 for 1.5 hours
Hot Stone Massage Placing warmed stones on certain areas of the body, such as acupressure points, the stones may be used as massage tools or be temporarily left in place. Used along with other massage techniques, hot stones can be quite soothing and relaxing as they transmit heat deep into the body. $65 for 1 hour
